Responsible Gambling

At In Play Tips, we believe betting should always be approached responsibly. While we provide analysis, insights, and educational betting content, we recognise that gambling can present risks for some individuals.

We encourage all members to treat betting as entertainment only and to stay in control of both time and money spent.

Responsible Gambling Guidelines

Set Financial Limits

Only wager money you can afford to lose. Establish a betting budget in advance and never exceed it.

Manage Your Time

Limit how much time you spend betting. Take regular breaks and ensure gambling does not interfere with work, relationships, or daily life.

Do Not Chase Losses

Losses are part of betting. Trying to recover losses by placing additional bets often leads to further harm.

Keep Betting Enjoyable

Betting should never be used as a way to make money, solve financial problems, or cope with stress. If it stops being enjoyable, it's time to step away.

Signs Gambling May Be a Problem

You or someone you know may be at risk if gambling is causing:

  • ▸Spending more money or time than intended
  • ▸Anxiety, frustration, or restlessness when not betting
  • ▸Hiding betting behaviour from friends or family
  • ▸Borrowing money or selling items to fund betting
  • ▸Neglecting work, personal responsibilities, or relationships

Recognising these signs early is important.
